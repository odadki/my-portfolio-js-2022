ABOUT

Who is Danielle?

A musician turned Front-End Developer who has an eye for UX Design.

I'm passionate about building on the intersection of UX Design and the engineering the code to make it happen. A highly functional design, user experience and elegant code structure all matter to me. I believe in the power of simplicity.

I have a BA in Arts from University of Hawai'i (Honolulu, Hawai'i is where I was born and raised!), a Certificate in UX Design from Coursera, a Certificate in Front-End Development from SheCodes and a Certificate in Web Development from Udemy's ZTM

I'm enjoy creating things, refining them, and making them better. I am great at working independently and with others, and am open to looking forward to working with a team that shares similar values.
